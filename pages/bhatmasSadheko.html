Bhatmas Sadheko

June 24, 2025

Bhatmas Sadheko is a popular Nepali snack made by mixing roasted soybeans with chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, coriander, and a blend of tangy spices. Bhatmas Saadheko is a favorite in Nepali homes, especially during picnics, gatherings, and as a quick snack with chiura. Its simplicity, bold flavor, and satisfying crunch make it a beloved part of the Nepali food tradition.

Prep Time

5 - 10 mins

Cook Time

5 - 7 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup soybeans (bhatmas)
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2-3 green chilies (chopped, adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon or 1 tablespoon vinegar (for tanginess)

Instructions:

Roast Soyabeans

  1. Dry roast the soybeans in a pan over medium heat for 4-5 minutes, stirring until they puff up slightly and are crunchy. Remove from heat.

Mix the spices

  1. In a bowl, combine the roasted soybeans with cumin powder, chili powder, salt, lemon juice (or vinegar), mustard oil and mix well

Serve

  1. Enjoy Bhatmas Saadheko with chiura, dal-bhat, or as a crunchy snack!

Pro Tips

  • Roasting the soybeans well is key for the best crunch.
  • Mustard oil gives authentic flavor.
  • You can add finely chopped onions or timur for extra Nepali touch.
Happy Cooking!
