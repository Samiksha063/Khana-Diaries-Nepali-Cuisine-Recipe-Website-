Saag(Spinach)

June 24, 2025

Spinach (Saag) is a nutritious green leafy vegetable widely used in Nepali cuisine. Often stir-fried with garlic, green chilies, and a hint of mustard oil, it's served as a healthy side dish with rice or dal.

Prep Time

5 - 7 mins

Cook Time

5 - 10 mins

Ingredients:

  • Fresh spinach (washed and chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil (for authentic flavor)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • 2-3 green chilies (whole or sliced, to taste)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Heat oil and temper spices

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan until lightly smoking.
  2. Add fenugreek seeds and let them turn dark brown.
  3. Add garlic and green chilies. Stir until garlic is golden.

Add saag

  1. Add spinach and stir well.
  2. Cook on medium-high heat for 2-4 minutes until wilted but still bright green.

Season and Serve

  1. Add salt to taste.
  2. Serve hot with dal-bhat or roti.

Pro Tips

  • Don't overcook. Keep saag fresh and green.
  • A pinch of turmeric or timur can be added for variety.
Happy Cooking!
