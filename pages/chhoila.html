Buff Chhoila

June 22, 2025

Buff Chhoila is a spicy and smoky Newari dish made from grilled or fried buffalo meat, marinated in mustard oil, garlic, ginger, and traditional Nepali spices. Buff Chhoila is often eaten during festivals, family gatherings, and special feasts. It is one of the main dishes served during Newar cultural events like Samay Baji (feast with beaten rice).

Prep Time

10 - 15 mins

Cook Time

20 - 25 mins

Ingredients:

  • 500g buffalo meat (boneless)
  • 4 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2-3 green chilies (chopped)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves to garnish

Instructions:

Cook the meat

  1. Boil or grill the buffalo meat until tender. Let it cool a bit and cut into small pieces.

Prepare the spice oil

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan.
  2. Add fenugreek seeds(methi) and let them turn black.
  3. Remove from heat and let the oil cool a little.

Mix the choila

  1. In a bowl, add the meat pieces.
  2. Add garlic paste, ginger paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, green chilies, lemon juice, and salt.
  3. Pour the warm oil with fenugreek seeds over the meat and mix everything well.

Serve

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and enjoy with beaten rice (chiura) and pickles.

Pro Tips

  • Adjust the chili level as you like. Buff Chhoila is usually spicy!
  • Use fresh mustard oil for a strong, authentic taste.
  • Let the Chhoila sit for a few minutes before serving so the flavors blend nicely.
Happy Cooking!
