Mula ko Achar(Radish pickle)

July 22, 2025

This is a quick and refreshing radish pickle made with raw, crisp radish tossed in mustard oil, turmeric, garlic, and chilies. Itâ€™s zesty, slightly pungent, crunchy, and made in under 15 minutes. This version is best eaten fresh, making it perfect as a side with dal bhat, roti, or chiura (beaten rice) during daily meals.

Prep Time

10 - 15 mins

Cook Time

0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium white radishes (medium diced)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste (or crushed garlic)
  • 1-2 fresh green chilies, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (or juice of 1 medium lemon)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon roasted sesame seeds (crushed)

Instructions:

  1. Wash, peel, dice the radish.
  2. Lightly squeeze to remove some water if it's too wet.
  3. In a bowl, combine radish, garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric powder, and salt.
  4. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Heat mustard oil just until warm (optional), then pour over the mixture.
  6. Add fenugreek seeds. Let them darken slightly.
  7. Add turmeric, chili powder, and the mashed lapsi to the oil.
  8. Toss everything together thoroughly.
  9. Garnish with crushed sesame seeds or coriander if using.

Pro Tips

  • Adjust sugar and salt to balance the lapsi's natural sourness.
  • Use mustard oil for authentic taste and preservation.
Happy Cooking!
