Tomato Pickle(Golveda ko Achar)

June 24, 2025

Tomato pickle made on a silauta (stone grinder) is a traditional Nepali tomato pickle is packed with natural flavor. Ripe tomatoes are roasted or boiled, then ground on the silauta with fresh garlic, ginger, green chilies, and salt. This method gives the achar a rich, earthy taste that no modern blender can match. It is perfect with dal-bhat, roti or dhindo.

Prep Time

10 - 15 mins

Cook Time

15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes (medium, juicy ones)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic (roughly chopped for grinding)
  • 2-3 green chilies (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Timur (Nepali Szechuan pepper)

Instructions:

  1. Roast tomatoes over open flame for smoky flavor, or boil until soft. Let them cool a little.
  2. On the silauta, grind garlic, ginger, and green chilies to a coarse paste.
  3. Add the softened tomatoes and mash/grind everything together until well mixed but still slightly chunky.
  4. Add salt and mix.
  5. Garnish with coriander.

Pro Tips

  • Roasting the tomatoes over fire gives the achar a beautiful smoky aroma.
  • Add roasted sesame seeds for regional variation.
  • If you like yo can also add paste of coriander and mints
Happy Cooking!
