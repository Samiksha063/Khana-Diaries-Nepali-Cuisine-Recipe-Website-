Prepare Your Favorite Nepali Dish At Home

Khana Diaries is a digital collection of traditional as well as modern Nepali recipes passed down through gerenation. Whether you are craving homemade Dal Bhat or Newari feasts, we have got you covered.

We understand that food is more than just substance, it's a connection to our roots, our memories, and our identity. Each recipe in our collection tells a story of families gathering around the kitchen, of festivals celebrated with special dishes, and of traditions lovingly maintained across generations.

Mission

Our mission is to document and preserve traditional recipes. We aim to inspire new generation of cooks to reconnect with their roots.

Vision

To reconnect Nepali communities and food lovers worldwide with the flavors, stories, and traditions of Nepali cuisine.

What can you expect from Khana Diaries?

The recipes in Khana Diaries consists of step by step instructions along with ingredients, stories connected with it and some traditional touch. There are recipes which has been passed down from generation to generation and we will provide you with the best and easiest way to cook so that you can enjoy the dish for yourself or your loved ones. Whether you are a student, food enthusiast, or want to explore Nepali dishes, at Khana Diaries, we celebrate the rich flavors and traditions of Nepali kitchen.
